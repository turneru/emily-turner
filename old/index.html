Emily & Turner

ARE GETTING MARRIED

Chicago, IL | June 18, 2022

After working at SmithBucklin together for over a year (albeit on different floors) and exchanging work related emails, we finally met at the company Christmas party in December 2017. At the after party Turner bought Emily a vodka lemonade (her favorite at the time), and they chatted for a good portion of the night. The queen of Irish goodbyes, Emily left before Turner asked for her number. Although a mutual friend and coworker offered to give him her number, he wanted to go the old fashioned route and ask for it himself. That night Turner went home and told his roommates at the time, that Emily was 'the one'.

He planned to bring her an orchid and leave a note at her desk early in the morning - not wanting to embarrass her in front of her team (or embarrass himself for that matter). He got to work and went to her floor expecting to find an empty desk, but little did he know Emily was an overachiever and got into work at 7:30 am most days. This turned out to a blessing in disguise. He gave her the orchid and told her how much he enjoyed talking at the party, and they exchanged numbers.

After texting back and forth for the next couple of days, Emily and Turner went on their first date that weekend, beginning with dinner at an Italian restaurant Turner believed was a mom-and-pop place in the neighborhood. Emily later informed him she had been to this restaurant before, and that it was more so a Chicago chain. Chain or mom-and-pop, it didn't matter - they hit it off and had a great time getting to know each other in between bites of pasta.

After dinner the date continued to Guthrie's Tavern, a local watering hole and dive bar with bookcases full of board games. They spent the next couple of hours battling it out in rounds of Yahteez!, Scrabble, and other board games. Turner of course let her win just enough times, though Emily disputes that fact and if it was even necessary (OJ is a word in Scrabble, right?). The date lasted about 6 hours total and Emily says thatâ€™s when she knew Turner was the one.

After a couple of years of dating, and in the midst of the mess of 2020, Emily and Turner took a quick trip to Cocoa Beach, FL for a weekend away. Turner proposed on the beach the morning after they arrived - he had planned with a family friend to help set up a blanket, champagne glasses, chilled champagne and flowers on the beach.

And as they say... the rest is history!

We can't wait to celebrate with all of our friends and family in June!. Thank you all s for being a part of our story.

Love,
Emily and Turner
