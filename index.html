Emily & Turner

Chicago, IL | June 18, 2022

 

After working at SmithBucklin together for over a year (albeit on different floors) and exchanging work related emails, Emily and Turner finally met at the office Christmas party in December 2017. At the after party Turner bought Emily a vodka lemonade, and they chatted for a good portion of the night. The queen of Irish goodbyes, Emily left before Turner asked for her number. Although a mutual friend offered him her number, he wanted to go the old fashioned route and ask for it himself. He planned to bring her an orchid and leave a note at her desk early in the morning - not wanting to embarass her in front of her team (or embarass himself for that matter). He dropped them off.
